about

i-CES stands for Innovative Computer Engineering Students' Society. The club was formally inaugurated on 2073/03/06 on presidentship of Ganesh Sapkota along with 12 executive members and 3 Advisor. i-CES is the official club of the Department of Computer & Electronics Engineering, Paschimanchal Campus. All the BCT students of Paschimanchal Campus are members of i-CES while executive committee of 13 members are selected as 3 from first year, 5 from second year and 5 from third year.

ICON vol-2

Icon is an annual journal published with the collection of final year project report memo of both final year BCT & BEX students of Paschimanchal Campus. The first Icon had around 50 copies printed which included final year projects and memo of BCT/069 batch . The Icon vol-2 had combined the final year project of both BCT/070 and BEX/070. It included a total of 16 projects in which 8 were from BCT and 8 were from BEX. Icon is all about giving the idea for the projects to the next batch students and enhancing the current final batch students to do better and prosper their thoughts on their projects.

  • No. Of Prints: 360
  • Inaugurated On : 2074/05/30
  • Inaugurated By: Department Of Electronics and Computer Engineering

Career Counselling

With the motive to inspire the generations of student in the Paschimanchal Campus, i-CES had organized a Career Counselling program with the help of guest speakers. Our speakers jhad talked about their in-hand experience in the real market and how to develop the abilities of students to be productive. This program was majorly targeted to fourth year students to know and understand the current trend and what follows as well as the new faces at Paschimanchal Campus too. To quote the program “Life is all about the 0s and the 1s. And their fluctuation.”

  • Speakers: Prabal Poudel (Phd. student at Otto-von-Guericke University) Santosh Bhandari (Ethical Hacker)
  • Participants : 118
  • Date: 2073/08/14

Code With Coffee Season 2

With great success in Code with Coffee season 1 in Paschimanchal Campus premises last year, the stage was set big this time for Code With Coffee Season 2. A regional level programme was held in Paschimanchal Campus attracting participants from the neighbouring colleges. The program was about coding for 4 hours in a team of 1 to 3 members for the solution of problem given to participants by our event host and drinking a lot coffee to recharge you throughout the program. This program was a pre-event to the “FSU Intertechnical Festival” held in the Paschimanchal Campus premises. The winner of this program was given Rs.1500 as winning prize and given a free entry to the “CodeCamp 2018.”

  • Participating Teams: 18
  • Winning Team Members: Roshan Rimal And Amar Nepali
  • Program Coordinator: Ganesh Tiwari , Bishwa Poudel & Amrit Ghimire
  • Program Date: 2075/03/26

CodeCamp 2018

The mega event of the FSU technical festival “CodeCamp 2018” was successfully conducted through Ashad 2-4,2074 by i-CES in collaboration with COTS Nepal. It was national level event in which the participants were not only from Pokhara but also from the capital Kathmandu. It was a hackathon on the theme “Human Aid” in which the various eager teams submitted their ideas and only a handful of 12 ideas were selected. The participating 12 teams were required to present their demo ideas during the final day of event by coding on first two days under mentorship which was judged by very insightful people of our field. There were also various prizes under many theme won by many teams.
